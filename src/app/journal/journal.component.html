Fonts used

On cover: Kumbh Sans on Google Fonts (Looks a lot like Futura). This handwritten font is Cedarville Cursive, also from Google Fonts.

Other facts

IMHO, bullet journals are a useful way of keeping track of life. I also really like the notebook style of the Origianl Kraft pocket notebooks from Field Notes, and I like a dot grid rule, and the A5 size (148x210mm); so this is a hybrid of all of that.

The background of the digital paper here has a dot grid background of 5mm (~18.9 pixels)
